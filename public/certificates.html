Jewellery Certifications & Authentication

Every item sold by Shop Anglo is independently verified and certified. Below you will find our certification standards, the laboratories we work with, and a record of each certified item currently available in our collection.

Certification Standards

  • GIA (Gemological Institute of America) – All diamonds 0.50 ct+ carry a GIA grading report covering the 4 Cs: Cut, Colour, Clarity, Carat.
  • AGS (American Gem Society) – Used for ideal-cut diamonds where angular precision is paramount.
  • Assay Office Hallmark – All gold (9ct, 18ct, 22ct, 24ct), silver (925), and platinum items bear an official Assay Office hallmark confirming metal purity.
  • ISO 9001 Quality Management – Our internal processes conform to ISO 9001 quality management standards.
  • CIBJO Compliance – We adhere to the International Jewellery Confederation's nomenclature and grading standards.
  • Kimberley Process – All diamonds are sourced in compliance with the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme (conflict-free).
