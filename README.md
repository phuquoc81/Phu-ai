## Security Features

Phu AI is built with strong security protections to keep users safe:

- **No Pop-Up Apps** — all intrusive pop-ups and pop-up applications are blocked and removed.
- **Malware Protection** — detects and removes malware before it can harm your system.
- **Virus Protection** — scans and eliminates viruses in real time.
- **Worm Protection** — prevents worms from spreading through your network.
- **Spyware Protection** — blocks spyware from collecting your personal information.
- **Anti-Phishing** — identifies and neutralises phishing attempts to protect your credentials.
